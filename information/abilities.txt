### Weapon Abilities
* Fire (Uncommon) - sets enemies on fire for 4 seconds.
* Frost (Uncommon) - enemies become frozen for 4 seconds.
* Poison (Uncommon) - enemies become poisoned for 7 seconds.
* Strength (Uncommon) - the user receives a strength buff for 5 seconds. Occurs 10% of the time.
* Elemental (Rare) - randomly chooses one of three different abilities and uses it (fire, frost, or poison).
* Darkness (Rare) - enemies become blinded for 5 seconds. Occurs 10% of the time.
* Light (Rare) - enemies become weakened for 5 seconds. Enemies also have a 10% chance of becoming blinded for 3 seconds.
* Bloodlust (Legendary) - enemies begin to bleed, dealing a randomized amount of damage.
* Ethereal (Legendary) - half the damage dealt is returned to the user as health.
* Sting (Ancient) - deals an extra 10 points of damage to the enemy.
* Void (Ancient) - a 5% chance of instantly killing the enemy.
